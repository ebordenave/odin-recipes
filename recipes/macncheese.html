Mac n' Cheese Recipe

Eddie's Mac n' Cheese Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. roll your dough
  2. spread tomato sauce liberally
  3. add all (or nearly all) the cheese in your fridge
  4. layer with large pepperoni slices
  5. add black pepper, garlic pepper, garlic cloves, crushed red pepper and chipotle sauce
  6. cook until your home smells delicious
